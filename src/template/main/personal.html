Politically exposed person status information

  • 1. Are you a US citizen, US green-card holder or by any other means US tax resident?

    Individuals who are or have been entrusted with prominent public functions by any country, for example: - Heads of State or of government, - senior politicians, - senior government, judicial or military officials, - senior executives of state owned corporations, - important political party officials, - persons who are or have been entrusted with a prominent function by an international organization, refers to members of senior management or individuals who have been entrusted with equivalent functions, i.e. directors, deputy directors and members of the board or equivalent functions.

  • 2. Are you family member of a politically exposed person in FATF definition?

    Individuals who are related to a PEP either directly (by consanguinity) or through marriage or similar (civil) forms of partnership. This category includes spouse or partner of a legal status, own child or child of a spouse or civil partner, parents, grandparents, grandchildren, siblings.

  • 3. Are you a close associate of a politically exposed person in FATF definition?

    Individuals who are closely connected to a PEP, either socially or professionally. It includes but not limited to being a shareholder of a commercial company with PEP or being an individual that owns a company operated in benefit of PEP (when it is known that PEP is a final beneficiary of the company).
